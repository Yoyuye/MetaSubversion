Failure to adhere to the Omniscope lab safety procedures may result in the exposure to extremely dangerous cultures.


LAB PROCEDURES:

All Cultures must be kept under 32 degrees farenheit, or 0 degrees celcius. Failure to do so results in either the death of the culture, or its rapid reproduction.

Employees must wear gloves and masks at all times. Failure to do so may result in infection, in which many cases may be highly contageous and fatal.

Lab equipment is cleaned twice a day. All lab enviormnents remain stable and sterile.

If any employee becomes ill or encounters a culture without the proper safety gear, they are to be quarantined immediately; if their conditions become unstable the employee must be eliminated, and the body burned.

If lab is compromised, break emergency glass and initiate self destruction sequence.
